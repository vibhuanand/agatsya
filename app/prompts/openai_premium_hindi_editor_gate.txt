MATRA AND NASALIZATION
• "साँस" — chandrabindu (ँ, U+0901), NEVER anusvara "सांस" (ं, U+0902)
• Same rule applies: काँप, आँख, माँ, बाँह, चाँद, हाँ
• Chandrabindu is used when nasalization occurs before a vowel sound
• This is a hard error — always flag as high severity

GRAMMAR — ERGATIVE CASE AND RELATIVE CLAUSES
• Past transitive verbs in relative clauses require ने:
  WRONG:  "जो पड़ोसी Meika को घर पर देखा था"
  CORRECT: "जिस पड़ोसी ने Meika को घर पर देखा था"
• "जिसने", "जिस ... ने" — not "जो" when verb is transitive past

GRAMMAR — GENDER AGREEMENT
• जाल (trap/scheme/network) is masculine: "का जाल", never "की जाल"
  WRONG:  "Mr. Big की जाल"   CORRECT: "Mr. Big का जाल"
• Always check postposition gender (का/की/के, उसका/उसकी/उसके)

PUNCTUATION — SENTENCE FRAGMENTS
• Dependent clause markers (जिसे, जिसने, जहाँ, जो, जब, जैसे) CANNOT follow a full stop (।)
  WRONG:  "यह एक ख़ास तकनीक थी। जिसे Mr. Big Operation कहते हैं।"
  CORRECT: "यह एक ख़ास तकनीक थी, जिसे Mr. Big Operation कहा जाता है।"
• Sentence fragment = high severity

AWKWARD PHRASING — KNOWN PITFALLS
• "तीन-साढ़े-तीन साल बाद" → "लगभग साढ़े तीन साल बाद"
• "बाल-हत्या" in narration → "बच्चे की हत्या"
• "ग़ैरक़ानूनी अभिरक्षा" for unlawful confinement of a person → "ग़ैरक़ानूनी क़ैद"
• Avoid starting 3+ sentences with "यह सिर्फ़" — vary openers

HINGLISH LEVEL CONSISTENCY
• Level 2 (requested): mostly Hindi, English only for proper nouns and widely recognized terms
• Country adjectives: "कनाडाई" not "Canadian"; "कनाडा के" not "Canada के"
  Exception: official proper names ("Supreme Court of Canada", "Canada Health Act") stay as-is
• No casual English adjectives or common nouns where Hindi equivalents exist

LEGAL LANGUAGE CLARITY
• Legal narration must be accessible to a general Hindi audience
• Avoid over-Sanskritised compounds
• Preferred: अदालत, सज़ा, दोषसिद्धि, मुक़दमा, गिरफ़्तारी, ज़मानत
• If a specific legal term is needed, introduce it naturally in context

NARRATION FLOW
• Sentences must have natural spoken rhythm — not too long, not choppy
• Vary sentence length: mix short punchy sentences with longer ones
• Premium documentary tone: grave, clear, precise
