NARRATIVE PACING AND REVEAL STRUCTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
These rules govern HOW the story reveals itself, not WHAT facts to include.

DELAYED REVELATION
• Do NOT place the final death, outcome, verdict, or killer identity in the opening section
  unless the case framing makes it absolutely unavoidable.
• The opening hook must be built around a question, a contradiction, a phone call, a moment
  of institutional failure, or an emotional turning point — not a summary of the outcome.
• Reveal major outcomes gradually and respectfully across sections.
• Do not hide facts dishonestly. Do not invent uncertainty where facts are confirmed.
  Pacing is about reveal order, not deception.

FIVE-ZONE REVEAL STRUCTURE
Map narrative_sections to these five zones and record zone in the schema field below:
  Zone 1 — First 10%:   setup, emotional stakes, central question. No outcome revealed.
  Zone 2 — 10–35%:      disappearance / initial call / first pressure. Escalation.
  Zone 3 — 35–65%:      investigation, failed decisions, media escalation, institutional tension.
  Zone 4 — 65–85%:      truth, legal consequences, verdict, perpetrator confirmed.
  Zone 5 — Final 15%:   aftermath, reforms, reflection, moral weight.

SUSPENSE DEVICES — USE THESE
Build suspense from institutional and procedural tension, not graphic detail:
  • Unanswered questions and timeline uncertainty
  • Failed institutional handovers and investigative errors
  • Media interference and escalating public pressure
  • Moral choices and ethical failures
  • Legal consequences and systemic exposure
Do NOT use graphic body details, child suffering, or injury descriptions as suspense devices.

OPENING HOOK QUESTION
central_question must frame ONE clear narrative question that drives the episode, such as:
  • "What went wrong that morning?"
  • "Why did a private family crisis become a national media storm?"
  • "How did a ransom case expose an entire system?"
  • "When every camera was watching, who was actually protecting the victim?"

ORIGINAL DOCUMENTARY STRUCTURE
• narrative_sections must reflect an original documentary structure — NOT the source transcript
  sequence. Do not preserve the source narrator's exact reveal order.
• Use fact_lock, emotional_details, and key_evidence_or_turning_points to create our own
  structure that serves the central_question.
