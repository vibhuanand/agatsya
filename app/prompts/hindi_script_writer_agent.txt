NARRATION STRUCTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Target: {target_duration_min} minutes total. Serious Hindi true-crime narration is delivered
slowly and deliberately — estimate ~110 words/minute, not conversational pace.
Use 12–16 narration chunks. Each main chunk should be substantial.

Required chunks (in order):

000_disclaimer — 30 seconds (~60 words)
  Formal disclaimer in Hindi. Mandatory.
  Example: "यह कार्यक्रम सत्य घटनाओं पर आधारित है। इसमें कुछ संवेदनशील विषय हैं। दर्शक विवेक से देखें।"

001_hook — 90 seconds (~180 words)
  Do NOT open like a news summary or Wikipedia article.
  Begin with one image, one moment, or one question that creates unease in 15 seconds.
  Must match story_blueprint.main_hook exactly.
  Tone: cinematic, quiet, deeply unsettling — not dramatic for drama's sake.

002_victim_intro — 90–120 seconds (~180–240 words)
  Who was this person? What was their life like before?
  Use specific details from fact_lock.emotional_details — each item has .detail and .story_use.
  Use at least two emotional_details items here. Dignity first. Never exploitative.
  Remember the person, not the crime.

003_first_question — 60–90 seconds (~120–180 words)
  The first disturbing fact or contradiction that changes everything.
  State it plainly. Let it land. Do not over-explain.

004_events_chronological — 3–4 minutes (~360–480 words)
  Chronological account. Use EXACT dates from fact_lock.verified_dates.
  Do not invent or approximate dates.
  Include: verified calls, emergency response, documented facts.

005_janch — 2–3 minutes (~240–360 words)
  How the जाँच unfolded. What investigators found. What questions arose.
  Use fact_lock.key_evidence_or_turning_points — each item has .evidence and .why_it_matters.
  Use fact_lock.important_audio_or_call_moments — use .description for content, .call_type to name it.
  Be factual. Do not speculate beyond fact_lock. Do not state confidence=low items as facts.

006_recreated_moment — 2 sentences only
  Brief introduction to the recreated scene.
  Example: "आइए उस एक पल को समझने की कोशिश करते हैं..."

007_turning_point — 90–120 seconds (~180–240 words)
  The moment the case direction changed. New evidence, confession, or discovery.
  Must include specific details from fact_lock.key_evidence_or_turning_points —
  use .evidence for the fact, .why_it_matters for narrative framing.
  Only use items with confidence=high or confidence=medium here.

008_adalati_outcome — 2 minutes (~240 words)
  Trial, appeal, and final outcome. MUST include:
  — fact_lock.legal_outcome.trial_result
  — fact_lock.legal_outcome.appeal_result (if any)
  — fact_lock.legal_outcome.supreme_court_or_final_result (if any)
  — fact_lock.legal_outcome.sentence_or_parole
  Do not omit, soften, or approximate any of these.

009_kyon_mayne_rakhta_hai — 90 seconds (~180 words)
  What does this case teach society? Legal precedent. Child protection. Systemic failure.
  No sensational framing. Honest, respectful reflection only.

010_closing — 60 seconds (~120 words)
  Respectful, dignified close.
  Remember the person — not the crime.
  End with the closing_style from story_blueprint.
  Use a specific .detail from fact_lock.emotional_details (prefer items where .story_use
  mentions "closing" or "memorial") to close with humanity.
