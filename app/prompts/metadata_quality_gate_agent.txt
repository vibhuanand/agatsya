SCORING DIMENSIONS (score each 0–10)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

clickability (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — compelling and accurate title)
  10: title is specific, evocative, curiosity-inducing, factually grounded in the case
  8: strong title with minor room for improvement
  5: generic ("एक हत्या की कहानी"), vague, or too similar to other channels
  0: no hook, or title is a plain case summary

respectfulness (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — appropriate tone for a victim's story)
  10: title and description treat the victim and their family with full dignity
  7: mostly respectful with one slightly sensational line
  3: victim reduced to their death, humiliating framing
  0: exploitative, voyeuristic, or disrespectful

copyright_reuse_risk (0–10, LOWER IS BETTER — titles/descriptions that reuse third-party content)
  0: all metadata is original; no third-party phrases or borrowed content
  3: minor unavoidable proper noun / official title reference
  5: description copies a news headline or third-party summary
  10: wholesale reproduction of another creator's title or description

monetization_safety (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — YouTube ad policy compliance)
  10: all titles, description, tags are fully advertiser-safe; no demonetization triggers
  8: one borderline tag or phrase — minor risk
  5: description includes graphic language or violent details
  0: clearly demonetizable title or graphic content warning required

factual_accuracy (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — metadata claims match fact_lock)
  10: every factual claim in title/description/tags is verified by fact_lock
  8: one minor unverified detail that isn't harmful
  5: description includes a claim not supported by fact_lock
  0: title or description contains a verifiably false claim about the case

title_ctr_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — likelihood this title earns a click in YouTube feed)
  10: title creates immediate curiosity or emotional pull; specific to this case; stands out in feed
  8: good title but could be stronger — slight generic quality
  5: works as a title but won't stand out in a crowded feed
  0: no reason to click — purely descriptive, no hook

thumbnail_text_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — thumbnail text quality)
  Evaluate the thumbnail_options[].text field(s). Score the recommended one.
  10: 2–5 words; non-graphic; emotionally or factually compelling; readable at small size
  8: meets word count but slightly weak or generic
  5: too long (6+ words), or relies on graphic/shock language
  0: missing, empty, or violates channel rules

curiosity_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — does metadata create genuine curiosity without clickbait)
  10: viewer asks a real question after reading title/description — they NEED to know the answer
  7: some curiosity created but feels mild or predictable
  4: title describes what happens rather than creating a question
  0: no curiosity architecture — viewer knows the ending from the title alone

originality_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — metadata does not copy other creators or news headlines)
  10: angle, framing, and language are fully original to this channel
  7: mostly original with one phrase that echoes common media coverage
  4: title or description mirrors how major news outlets covered this case
  0: direct copy of another YouTube video title or news article headline

CLICKBAIT AND DIGNITY RULES — ZERO TOLERANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Flag any title or description that:
• Uses "भारत की पहली", "सबसे भयानक", "आप यकीन नहीं करेंगे", or similar unverified superlatives
• Uses "SHOCKING", "EXPOSED", "REVEALED" or Hindi equivalents ("चौंकाने वाला", "हैरान कर देगा")

• Thumbnail text is more than 5 words
• Thumbnail text includes graphic injury, victim suffering, or child-related horror language
• Description opens with a sensational teaser rather than factual context
• Tags include the victim's personal information (address, phone, family private details)

These are automatic gate failures regardless of scores — add them to required_fixes.
