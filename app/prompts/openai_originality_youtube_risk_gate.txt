copying_risk_score (0–10, LOWER IS BETTER — degree of copying from source)
  0: fully original — source only provides facts, everything else is new
  3: minor structural similarity — unavoidable for a factual retelling
  5: some sections clearly follow source phrasing structure
  8: script reads like a translation of the source
  10: direct translation or reproduction

reused_content_risk_score (0–10, LOWER IS BETTER — unattributed third-party reuse)
  0: all content original or properly attributed
  5: substantial unlabelled borrowed content
  10: clear plagiarism of another creator's work

copyright_risk_score (0–10, LOWER IS BETTER — IP exposure)
  0: no copyrighted material reproduced without transformation
  5: song lyrics quoted, news text paraphrased very closely
  10: direct reproduction of copyrighted content

transformative_value_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — original contribution)
  10: deep original narrative, Hindi cultural context, emotional humanization
  5: mostly fact retelling with minimal editorial addition
  0: pure translation with no added value

youtube_ad_safety_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — advertiser safety)
  10: fully ad-safe — factual, dignified, no triggers
  7: one minor borderline element
  3: significant risk (graphic language, exploitative framing)
  0: clearly demonetizable

recreated_audio_safety_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — fictional dialogue safety)
  10: all scenes labelled, clearly fictional, safety notes present
  5: labelled but dialogue blurs fact/fiction
  0: unlabelled — serious misrepresentation risk
  (if no recreated scenes: 10)

metadata_safety_score (0–10, HIGHER IS BETTER — title/description/tags safety)
  10: original, specific, factual, ad-safe
  5: slightly clickbaity or borrowed phrasing
  0: graphic title or borrowed description
