labelling_compliance (0–10)
  10: every scene has label_on_screen="फिर से रचा गया संवाद" and not_original_audio=true
  5: labels present but one is incorrect or missing

factual_consistency (0–10)
  10: all dialogue is consistent with verified facts in fact_lock; no invented facts stated as real
  8: minor speculative line clearly framed as imagined
  5: dialogue contradicts or exaggerates a verified fact
  0: dialogue states false facts as real conversation

emotional_authenticity (0–10)
  10: scenes feel true to the human characters involved, emotionally grounded, not melodramatic
  8: mostly authentic with minor overreach
  5: scenes feel theatrical, stereotyped, or exploitative
  0: scenes are gratuitous, humiliating to victim, or sensationalist

victim_dignity (0–10)
  10: victim and their family are portrayed with full dignity and respect
  5: victim's suffering is implied rather than shown, minor dignity concern
  0: victim portrayed in a humiliating or voyeuristic way

dialogue_naturalness (0–10)
  10: Hindi dialogue sounds natural, conversational, and appropriate to the speaker's character
  8: mostly natural with minor stilted lines
  5: written Hindi, formal register, or implausible dialogue
  0: clearly fabricated speech that sounds nothing like a real person

overall_quality (0–10, holistic view across all scenes)
